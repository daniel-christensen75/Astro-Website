Venus

Venus is the second closest planet to the sun. While it is not the closest, it is the hottest with an average temperature of 864 degrees Fahrenheit (462 degrees Celsius). This is because of Venus' thick atmosphere which is mostly made up of the greenhouse gas carbon dioxide. Venus is so hot that many spacecraft have melted shortly after landing on the surface. Our neighboring planet has clouds like Earth does, but they are made up of sulfur instead of water vapor. Some have thought about trying to colonize Venus by landing large balloons on the dense clouds have a cloud city. However, humans would still not be able to breath on the planet, and it would be more challenging than colonizing our other neighboring planet, Mars.
